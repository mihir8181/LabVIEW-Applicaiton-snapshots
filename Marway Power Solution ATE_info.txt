Marway ATE Applications GUI


Designed GUI and Developed an application in LabView for calibration and continuous monitoring of 3Phase parameters over I2C protocol.
*Used xdimax's SUB-20 Multi Interface USB Adapter, Which is a I2C to USB converter. 
*Application met customers following requirements like,
a) Phase selection and configuration of their card through application,
b) Phase calibration,
c) Phase Parameter monitoring
d) Fetch card status, etc.
